# Problem Statement

## Background

Every year, thousands of students, fresh graduates, and job seekers become victims of fake job offers and fraudulent recruiters.

Scammers pretend to be HR representatives from well-known companies, send fake offer letters, and ask candidates to pay registration fees, training fees, or document verification charges.

Many candidates trust these recruiters because they do not have an easy way to verify whether a job offer or recruiter is genuine.

## Our Solution

JobShield AI is an AI-powered Job Verification Assistant.

Instead of trusting a recruiter immediately, users can first verify the job using our system.

The AI analyzes the job description, recruiter details, and common scam patterns to:

- Generate a Trust Score
- Detect scam indicators
- Explain why a job is suspicious
- Provide safety advice
- Suggest questions to ask the recruiter before proceeding

Our goal is to help users **Verify Before They Apply**.
