# 🛡️ JobShield AI

## AI-Powered Fake Job Detection and Recruiter Verification System

### Problem Statement
Thousands of students and job seekers become victims of fake recruiters and job scams. Many scams involve fake HR representatives, registration fees, unrealistic salary offers, and fraudulent interview processes.

### Our Solution
JobShield AI helps users verify job offers before applying by using Artificial Intelligence to detect scam indicators and provide evidence-based recommendations.

### Features
- 📄 Analyze Job Description
- 📷 Analyze Job Screenshot
- 🤖 AI Trust Score
- 🚩 Scam Evidence Detection
- 🛡️ Recruiter Verification
- 💡 AI Safety Advice
- ❓ Questions to Ask HR
