# Fake Job Examples

## Fake Job 1

Company Name Used: Amazon

Role: Data Entry Operator

Location: Work From Home

Experience: Fresher

Salary: ₹90,000/month

Interview: No

Registration Fee: ₹500

Official Email: amazonhr@gmail.com

Official Website: Not Available

Scam Type: Advance Fee Scam

Why is this fake?

❌ Uses a Gmail account instead of an official company email.
❌ Requests a registration fee before hiring.
❌ No interview process.
❌ Unrealistically high salary for a data entry role.
