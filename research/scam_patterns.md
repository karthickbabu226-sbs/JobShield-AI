# Scam Patterns Database

| ID | Scam Pattern | Risk | Why it's suspicious |
|----|--------------|------|---------------------|
| 1 | Registration fee | High | Genuine companies usually don't charge candidates. |
| 2 | No interview | High | Most companies interview candidates before hiring. |
| 3 | Gmail/Yahoo email | High | Official recruiters usually use company domains. |
| 4 | Unrealistic salary | Medium | Salary is much higher than normal. |
| 5 | WhatsApp only | Medium | Official communication is usually through company email. |
| 6 | Immediate joining | Medium | Used to pressure candidates. |
| 7 | Poor grammar | Low | Scam messages often contain spelling mistakes. |
| 8 | Limited vacancies | Medium | Creates urgency to rush candidates. |
| 9 | 100% job guarantee | High | No genuine company guarantees selection. |
| 10 | Training fee | High | Asking for money before hiring is a major red flag. |
